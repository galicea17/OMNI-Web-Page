OMNI: Never Be Lost In Translation

What we Are?
Have you ever been to a foriegn country and have no idea what any sign means? Are you visually impaired and have trouble reading text? Then OMNI is the app for you.

What we do

OMNI (Optical Mobile Narrative Interface) is an iOS app that recognizes an object through an iphone's camera, translates object name into desired language, and dictates text in desired language.
